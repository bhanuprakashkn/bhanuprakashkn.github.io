Current Work at CIRC NUS Jan 2025 – Present

Lead, Quantitative Imaging & Data-Driven Modelling — Clinical Imaging Research Centre (CIRC), National University of Singapore.
Over the past year, our team has built three research-grade, clinically oriented AI platforms that translate quantitative imaging into actionable clinical insight.

🔬

FUSION — Framework for Unified Spatial Integration of ONcologic Imaging

End-to-end platform for joint PET/CT and PET/MR analysis in oncology. Automated tumour segmentation and co-registration across multimodal volumes, with quantitative lesion characterisation — SUV metrics, volumetrics, and spatial distribution maps — for longitudinal treatment response assessment.

PET/CT · PET/MR Tumour Segmentation Multimodal Registration SUV Quantification ✓ Operational at CIRC NUS
🧬

COMPAS — COMPosition & Phenotype Analysis System

Automated body composition quantification from CT and MRI: visceral and subcutaneous fat, skeletal muscle, intra-muscular (IMAT) and inter-muscular fat (EMAT). Phenotype classification pipeline for metabolic profiling at population scale, designed for sarcopenia and oncology cohort studies.

Body Composition AI VAT · SAT · IMAT · EMAT Semantic Segmentation Radiomics ✓ Deployed in Cohort Studies
🧠

NEXUS — Neuro-Cardiac EXplorer for Dynamic Scanning

Full kinetic modelling pipeline for dynamic PET data: time-activity curve (TAC) extraction, Patlak and Logan graphical analyses, and parametric map generation (Ki maps, DVR, net influx rate). Integrates with NEURODESK (Queensland Brain Institute) for standardised neuro and cardiac dynamic PET workflows.

Dynamic PET Kinetic Modelling Patlak · Logan NEURODESK Integration ✓ Operational at CIRC NUS

Flagship Projects A*STAR · 2003–2024

Every project below was validated in a clinical or research environment. These are not demos.

🧠

Stroke Clinical AI System (iStrokeSuite)

End-to-end stroke CAD covering ischemic, hemorrhagic, probabilistic, and emergency room workflows. Automated segmentation of infarcted brain tissue from DWI/CT volumes, integrated with clinical PACS. Deployed across Singapore health clusters.

DWI Segmentation Atlas Integration PACS Deployment ✓ Clinical Deployment ✓ Asian Innovation Award Finalist
🧬

SG Normative Brain Template & Volumetry Atlas

Deep learning framework to construct a Singapore-specific ageing brain reference template — the first population-normalised volumetry atlas for an Asian cohort. Corrects the systematic bias in Western-trained clinical tools applied to Asian patients.

Deep Learning Brain Segmentation Population Health ✓ NHIC Innovation Grant ✓ Active at NUS CIRC
🔬

Schizophrenia Subtype Classification

ML pipeline for classification and subtyping of schizophrenia from structural and functional MRI. Unsupervised clustering identified biologically meaningful patient subgroups enabling personalised treatment pathways.

SVM / Clustering Neuroimaging Features Clinical Translation ✓ Peer-Reviewed Publication
🏃

AI Framework: Sarcopenic Obesity & Metabolic Profiling

Multimodal clinical AI pipeline for automated body composition analysis and metabolic phenotyping in Singapore's geriatric population. Designed for integration with national ageing-in-place clinical programmes.

Multi-modal Fusion Geriatric AI Body Composition ✓ Clinical Integration
🫀

Multi-site Cardiac MRI Analysis Application

Cross-site cardiac MRI application supporting diverse study types and acquisition protocols. Standardised workflows across research and clinical sites for reliable multi-centre cardiac studies.

Cardiac Segmentation Multi-site Pipeline DICOM Compliance ✓ Multi-centre Deployment

Brain Atlas Landmark Registration

Novel anatomical landmark detection for anterior/posterior commissures with geometrical fitting for improved atlas alignment. Adopted in multi-centre neuroimaging studies and protected through multiple international patents.

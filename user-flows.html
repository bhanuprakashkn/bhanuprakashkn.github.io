🖥️
PACS / Workstation Layer
DICOM input auto-routed to iStrokeSuite. AI overlay rendered directly on standard PACS viewer — no separate application launch needed. Designed to integrate into existing radiologist workflow without friction.
👨‍⚕️
Radiologist — Primary Output Consumer
Colour-coded lesion overlay with confidence score and volume measurement. One-click confirmation or correction. Optimised interpretability of outputs: result is a validated visual annotation, not raw data. Cognitive load reduced — radiologist confirms rather than searches.
🧠
Neurologist — Clinical Report Consumer
Structured report with stroke type, territory, volume, urgency flag, and treatment eligibility note. Delivered to neurologist workstation automatically on confirmation. Language calibrated to clinical decision vocabulary.
🚨
Emergency Physician — Triage Score Consumer
Single-screen triage output: stroke type · urgency level · time window flag. Designed for rapid consumption under emergency conditions — no scrolling, no ambiguity. Reduced cognitive load in decision-making at point of highest clinical stress.
📊
Audit / Quality System
All AI outputs, radiologist corrections, and timing data logged. Feeds retraining pipeline. Enables continuous improvement loop and supports clinical governance requirements.

🧠
Neurologist — Primary Consumer
Receives a deviation chart showing patient brain volumes compared to age/sex-matched Singapore normative range. Optimised interpretability of outputs — percentile visualisation, not raw mm³. Clinician immediately understands how unusual the finding is.
👴
Geriatric Clinician — Longitudinal Monitor
Serial scans compared against the same patient's prior baseline and the population trajectory. Cognitive decline tracked quantitatively — reduced reliance on subjective clinical impression alone.
📈
Population Health Researcher
Aggregated volumetry data from the Singapore cohort feeds back into the normative atlas, refining it over time. Designed as a self-improving reference system, not a static tool.

👩‍⚕️
Geriatrician — Primary Consumer
Receives phenotype classification with body composition visualisation (colour-coded compartments) and risk score. Optimised interpretability of outputs — results are expressed in clinical terms (high/moderate/low risk + intervention priority), not model probabilities.
🥗
Dietitian — Intervention Planner
Receives body fat distribution breakdown and metabolic profile to design targeted nutritional intervention. Cognitive load reduced: automated care plan draft is provided for review, not a blank-page task.
📋
Population Health Team — Aggregate Insights
Cohort-level phenotype distribution enables national programme planning. Singapore's ageing-in-place teams receive de-identified aggregate phenotype reports to plan resource allocation.
